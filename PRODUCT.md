# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Travelers looking to consolidate their trip planning across flights, trains, hotels, and attractions. They are overwhelmed by managing a million open tabs and want a frictionless, centralized hub.

## Product Purpose
PlanBro (Tripal) removes the friction from travel planning by aggregating transport, places, and hotels into one visually stunning experience. It ranks options in real-time based on comfort, safety, and budget, providing a seamless itinerary builder and live budget tracking ("The Purse"). Success is a seamless end-to-end trip booking flow.

## Brand Personality
The landing page feels premium, high-tech, and adventurous (smooth 3D motion). The app/dashboard feels calm and effortless to use, yet vibrant, energetic, and playful enough to feel alive.

## Anti-references
Dull, sterile, or "AI-generated" templates. Standard SaaS defaults (like the cream/sand monoculture), identical repetitive card grids, or anything that feels "dead".

## Design Principles
- Frictionless Consolidation: Aggregate complex real-time data into a single, clean view without visual clutter.
- Dynamic but Calm: Use motion and vibrant colors to make the interface feel alive, while keeping the core planning workflow calm and easy to digest.
- High-Tech yet Human: Leverage advanced algorithms and parallel processing under the hood, but present the UI warmly and playfully so it doesn't feel like a rigid terminal.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Provide elegant crossfades and instant transitions for users preferring reduced motion. No strict WCAG compliance is required at this stage.
